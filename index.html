About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Tel Aviv University, under the supervision of Oren Barkan and Noam Koenigstein. My research is centered on Interpretable and Explainable AI (XAI), trying to make complex black-box models more transparent and adaptive.

Prior to my academic career, I gained extensive experience as a Data Engineer, building large-scale data systems and services, focusing on data streaming, modeling, and ETL. Currently, I also manage a vibrant research community at my workplace, bridging the gap between theoretical AI and practical implementation.

I'm always happy to connect! Feel free to reach out if you'd like to explore potential collaborations.
